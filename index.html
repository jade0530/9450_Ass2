CALL FOR ABSTRACTS

Submission guidelines

Authors are encouraged to submit extended abstracts (2 pages) of original papers.
Areas of interest include but are not limited to:

  • coding and classification systems for medical data
  • messaging standards

Papers must be submitted in PDF format and forwarded via email (refer to Contact Us section).
Submissions not meeting the submission guidelines risk rejection without consideration of their merits.

Submission deadline: 5pm 18 October 2023
